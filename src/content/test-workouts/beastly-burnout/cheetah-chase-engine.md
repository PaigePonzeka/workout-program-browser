This wild Engine day amplifies stamina and speed with mixed cardio, hops, and animal agility for fat-burning fun — no heavy lifts, just pure animal fitness.
## Warm-Up
6 min alternating:
- 1 min easy row
- 12 jumping jacks
- 10 bodyweight good mornings

## Metcon
20 min AMRAP:
- 10 burpees
- 15 wall balls (20/14 lbs)
- 200m run/row/bike
- 20 alternating DB snatches (30/20 lbs)
- 12 box jump overs (20")

Scaling:
- Step back burpees, reduce wall ball or DB weight, sub step-ups
- Advanced: 25/20 wall balls, 40/25 DB, higher box

## Cool-Down
- Standing quad stretch: 45s/side
- Cat-cow: 1 min
- Seated forward fold: 1 min

## Core / Optional Finisher
Tabata (4 min):
- Alternating mountain climbers and plank holds

## Coaching Notes
Keep up that cheetah pace — consistent, controlled, but always fast. Pick movements/scaling to keep moving with short rest.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Standard, AMRAP 20 min
- Week 2: Increase wall balls to 18, 22 min AMRAP
- Week 3: Use heavier DB and try to minimize rests, AMRAP 20 min
- Week 4: Reduce to 14 min for intensity, use week 1 weights
