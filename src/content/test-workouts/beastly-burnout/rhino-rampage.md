A lower body stampede unleashing squats and posterior chain patterns to chase animal-level power and set the base for relentless calorie burning.
## Warm-Up
5 min treadmill incline walk
Then:
- 8 bodyweight squats + pause at bottom
- 10 banded glute bridges
- 12 alternating lunge & twist

## Strength or Skill
Back Squat
- 4x8 @ RPE 7
Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
- 3x12
Split Squat (DB or bodyweight)
- 3x10/leg
Rest 2 min between sets

## Metcon
15 min EMOM:
- Minute 1: 14 goblet squats (50/30 lbs)
- Minute 2: 12 box step-ups (24/20", alternating legs)
- Minute 3: 20 alternating reverse lunges
Continue for 5 rounds (15 min)

Scaling:
- Use lighter dumbbell, reduce height to small box/bench, cut reps to 8-10 as needed
- Advanced: Add 10-lb vest or hold DBs for step-ups

## Cool-Down
- Half-kneeling hip flexor: 1 min/side
- Prone quad stretch: 1 min/side
- Seated toe touch: 1 min

## Core / Optional Finisher
2 rounds:
- 15 heel taps
- 30s glute bridge hold

## Coaching Notes
Power up, but don’t charge blindly — low back neutral, knees track toes. Go animal, not reckless.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Squat 4x8, 15 min EMOM
- Week 2: 4x9 squat (add weight), 16 min EMOM (add 2 reps per move)
- Week 3: 5x6 heavy, 15 min EMOM (increase DB)
- Week 4: 3x10 deload, 12 min EMOM
