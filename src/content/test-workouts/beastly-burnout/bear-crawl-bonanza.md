A full-body, bite-sized blend of functional moves to finish the week strong, sharpening overall animal athleticism without overloading any one zone.
## Warm-Up
4 min alternating:
- 30s bike or jump rope
- 10 arm hugs + 10 air squats
Then:
- 12 alternating thread the needles

## Strength or Skill
Kettlebell Deadlift
- 3x12 @ moderate weight
Incline Push-Up
- 3x15 (feet on ground or on box)
Single-Arm Dumbbell Row
- 3x10/arm
Rest 60s between

## Metcon
12 min EMOM:
- Minute 1: 25s bear crawl (for distance)
- Minute 2: 16 Russian KB swings (moderate/heavy)
- Minute 3: 12 alternating reverse lunges with DBs (25/15 lbs)

Scaling:
- Crawl on knees, reduce lunge weight, limit KB to light
- Advanced: Crawl length, heavier KB, increase reps

## Cool-Down
- Child’s pose: 1 min
- Seated glute stretch: 1 min/side
- Shoulder sweep: 1 min/side

## Core / Optional Finisher
2 rounds:
- 20 flutter kicks
- 30s side plank/side

## Coaching Notes
Think total-body quality, not max load. Alligator your way through every rep with control, especially on core vol.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Standard, EMOM 12 min
- Week 2: Add 2 reps to swings/lunges, EMOM 14 min
- Week 3: Increase KB/DB weight, EMOM 12 min
- Week 4: Reduce to 10 min, focus on speed/technique
