A push-focused workout that roars through chest, shoulders, and triceps, setting the tone for animal-strong pressing and the courage to attack calories.
## Warm-Up
5 min jog or row (easy pace)
Then 2 rounds for quality:
- 8 banded shoulder dislocates
- 10 arm swings (cross-body & overhead)
- 10 push-ups to down dog

## Strength or Skill
Barbell Bench Press
- 4x8 @ RPE 7
Standing Dumbbell Shoulder Press
- 3x10 @ RPE 7
Alternating DB Skullcrushers
- 3x12 per arm
Rest 90s between sets

## Metcon
16 min AMRAP:
- 12 alternating dumbbell snatch (40/25 lbs)
- 10 deficit push-ups (hands on DBs)
- 14 box jumps (24/20")

Scaling:
- Use lighter dumbbell for snatches, incline push-ups for deficit, step-ups for box jumps
- Advanced: Use 50/35 lbs and add elevated push-ups

## Cool-Down
- Chest doorway stretch: 1 min/side
- Overhead triceps stretch: 45s/arm
- Child’s pose: 90s

## Core / Optional Finisher
3 rounds:
- 12 V-ups
- 25s plank hold

## Coaching Notes
Hunt for control on pressing and smooth snatches in the metcon. Keep standards strong — elbows lock, chest up on jumps. 
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Bench 4x8, AMRAP 16 min
- Week 2: Bench 4x8 (add 5 lbs), AMRAP 18 min
- Week 3: Bench 5x6 heavier, AMRAP 16 min (increase DB weight/box height)
- Week 4: Bench 3x10 @ lighter load (deload), AMRAP 12 min
