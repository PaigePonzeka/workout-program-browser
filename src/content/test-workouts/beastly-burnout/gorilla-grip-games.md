A back-and-biceps pulling pursuit, loaded with horizontal and vertical tugs to cultivate gorilla-level grip and functional strength under the wild theme.
## Warm-Up
6 min bike or jump rope (moderate)
Then:
- 10 band pull-aparts
- 12 bent-over PVC rows
- 8 scapular lat pull-downs

## Strength or Skill
Barbell Bent-Over Row
- 4x8 @ RPE 7
Lat Pull-Down (Wide Grip)
- 4x10
Weighted Supine Hold (Dead Hang from pull-up bar or banded)
- 3x20s
Rest 90s between sets

## Metcon
14 min AMRAP:
- 12 single-arm DB row (50/30)
- 10 banded kneeling lat pull-downs
- 30 sec farmer’s carry (2 DBs or KBs, as heavy as can handle)

Scaling:
- Use lighter weights/bands as needed, incline DB row for support
- Advanced: add reps/farmers carry distance

## Cool-Down
- Seated lat stretch: 1 min/side
- Cross-body arm stretch: 45s/arm
- Neck stretch: 30s/side

## Core / Optional Finisher
3 rounds:
- 15 hollow rocks
- 30s suitcase hold (single KB/DB R side/round 1, L round 2, then both)

## Coaching Notes
Go beast-mode but keep the lower back safe — stay packed in every row. Farmer’s carries build real strength!
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Row 4x8, AMRAP 14 min
- Week 2: Row 4x9, AMRAP 16 min (add load on DB row)
- Week 3: Row 5x7 heavier, AMRAP 14 min (longer carries)
- Week 4: Row 3x10 light, AMRAP 10 min (deload)
