  ## Program Overview 
  A four-week, tournament-inspired CrossFit program where every session is a round closer to your fitness 'final four!' Blend fun, bracket-style metcons with structured strength training for full-court fat loss, serious conditioning, and strength maintenance. Themed metcons mimic the intensity of March competition—upping variety and energy while ensuring safe and progressive training week to week.
