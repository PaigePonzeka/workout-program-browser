## Warm-Up (8 min)

- Band pull-aparts: 2 × 15
- Push-ups: 2 × 8
- Scapular pull-ups: 2 × 8
- Empty bar bench press: 2 × 8 (progressive loading)

## Main Work

### A1. Barbell Bench Press (Heavy)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 5 | 3–5 | 3–4 min | 8–9 |

**Cues**: Wide grip. Arch back slightly. Unrack with locked arms. Control down, explode up.

### B1. Weighted Pull-Ups

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 4–6 | 3 min | 8–9 |

**Cues**: Add weight via belt or dumbbell between feet. Full dead hang. Pull hard. Chin over bar.

### B2. Barbell Overhead Press

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 4–6 | 3 min | 8–9 |

**Cues**: Strict press — no leg drive. Bar starts at collarbone. Press in slight arc. Lock out overhead.

### C1. Barbell Row (Pendlay)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 5–6 | 2 min | 8 |

**Cues**: Bar returns to floor each rep. Explosive pull. Flat back. Elbows drive past the body.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Chest doorway stretch: 45 seconds
- Lat hang: 30 seconds
- Shoulder cross-body stretch: 30 seconds per side
