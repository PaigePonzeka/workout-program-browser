## Warm-Up (8 min)

- Jump rope: 2 × 30 seconds
- Bodyweight squats: 1 × 10
- Push-ups: 1 × 10
- Box jumps (low): 2 × 5
- Empty bar power cleans: 1 × 8

## Main Work

### A1. Power Clean

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 5 | 3 | 3 min | 8 |

**Cues**: Start position like deadlift. Explosive hip extension. Catch in front squat position. Reset each rep.

### B1. Push Press

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 4–5 | 2–3 min | 8 |

**Cues**: Small dip, explosive drive. Use leg power to get bar moving. Lock out overhead.

### B2. Box Jumps

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 5 | 90 sec | 7 |

**Cues**: Step down, don't jump down. Full hip extension at top. Land soft. Reset between reps.

### C1. Kettlebell Swing

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 15 | 60 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Hip hinge, not squat. Explosive hip snap. Arms are just along for the ride. Squeeze glutes at top.

### C2. Barbell Row (Explosive)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 6–8 | 90 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Moderate weight. Pull fast. Touch at lower chest. Controlled lower.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Cat-cow: 10 reps
- World's greatest stretch: 5 per side
- Child's pose: 30 seconds
- Deep breathing: 1 minute
