## Warm-Up (10 min)

- Bodyweight squats: 2 × 15
- Hip circles: 10 per direction
- Glute bridges: 2 × 12
- Empty bar squats: 2 × 8 (progressive loading)

## Main Work

### A1. Barbell Back Squat (Heavy)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 5 | 3–5 | 3–5 min | 8–9 |

**Cues**: Low bar position. Brace hard. Break at hips first. Drive out of the hole. Keep chest up.

### B1. Conventional Deadlift

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 3–5 | 3–4 min | 8–9 |

**Cues**: Feet hip-width. Mixed or hook grip. Push floor away. Bar drags up shins. Lockout with glutes.

### C1. Front Squat

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 5–6 | 2–3 min | 8 |

**Cues**: Elbows high. Upright torso. Full depth. Drive through mid-foot.

### C2. Barbell Hip Thrust

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 8–10 | 2 min | 8 |

**Cues**: Upper back on bench. Chin tucked. Drive through heels. Squeeze glutes hard at top.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Pigeon pose: 45 seconds per side
- Standing quad stretch: 30 seconds per side
- Hamstring stretch: 30 seconds per side
