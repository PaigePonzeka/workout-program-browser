  ## Program Overview
  Frostbite Furnace is a winter-themed, CrossFit-inspired 4-week program focused on melting away fat with fiery conditioning and heat-building strength. Each session ignites metabolism with high-energy circuits and progressive strength, while chilly-named workouts keep things light-hearted and motivating all month long.
