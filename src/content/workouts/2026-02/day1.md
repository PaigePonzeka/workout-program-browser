  ## Warm-Up

500m row or 3 min jump rope
2 rounds:
- 10 scap pull-ups
- 12 banded face pulls
- 10 prone Y raises

## Strength or Skill

Weighted Pull-Ups (or bodyweight)
- 4 sets x 5-7 reps @ RPE 8
- Rest 2-3 min

Bent Over Barbell Rows
- 4 sets x 8 reps @ RPE 7

Single Arm Dumbbell Hammer Curl
- 3 sets x 12/arm

## Metcon

"Icy Ladder" (for time, cap 16 min):
10-8-6-4-2
- Pull-ups
- Kettlebell swings (53/35#)
- Goblet reverse lunges per leg

Scaling: Band-assisted pull-ups; lighter KB; Split lunges as needed

## Cool-Down

- Hanging lat stretch: 60 sec
- Standing biceps wall stretch: 45 sec/side
- Cat-cow x 1 min

## Core / Optional Finisher

2 rounds:
- 30 sec suitcase hold/side (heavy DB)
- 20 lying windshield wipers

## Coaching Notes

Choose strict pull-ups over kipping. Rows: squeeze at the top. Aim for quality movement in the metcon, especially as grip fatigue sets in.

### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Sets/reps as written, 16 min cap
- Week 2: Add set or small band weight, tighter time cap
- Week 3: Increase pull-up challenge or add row weight, remove band if possible
- Week 4: 3x8 rows @ lower load, 30% less volume in metcon
