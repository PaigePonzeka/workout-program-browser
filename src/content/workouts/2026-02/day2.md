## Warm-Up (8 min)

- Cat-cow stretches: 1 × 10
- Band pull-aparts: 2 × 15
- Dead hangs: 2 × 15 seconds
- Light lat pulldowns: 1 × 15

## Main Work

### A1. Barbell Bent-Over Row

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 6–8 | 2–3 min | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Hinge at hips, 45° torso angle. Pull to lower sternum. Squeeze shoulder blades at top. Controlled lowering.

### A2. Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldown)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 8–10 | 90 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Full dead hang at bottom. Initiate with lats, not arms. Chin over bar. Add weight if bodyweight is easy.

### B1. Seated Cable Row

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 10–12 | 90 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Neutral grip. Chest tall. Squeeze at the back. 2-second negative.

### B2. Face Pulls

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 15–20 | 60 sec | 7 |

**Cues**: High cable attachment. Pull to ears. External rotate at the end. Hold for a count.

### C1. Incline Dumbbell Curl

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 10–12 | 60 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: 45° bench. Let arms hang fully. Supinate at the top. No swinging.

### C2. Hammer Curls

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 2 | 12–15 | 60 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: Neutral grip throughout. Controlled tempo. Squeeze at top.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Child's pose: 30 seconds
- Lat stretch (doorway): 30 seconds per side
- Bicep wall stretch: 30 seconds per arm
- Deep breathing: 1 minute
