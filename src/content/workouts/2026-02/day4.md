## Warm-Up (8 min)

- Jumping jacks: 1 × 30
- Inchworms: 1 × 8
- Bodyweight squats: 1 × 12
- Band pull-aparts: 1 × 15
- Arm circles: 30 seconds each direction

## Main Work

### A1. Deadlift (Conventional)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 5–6 | 3 min | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Feet hip-width. Grip just outside knees. Chest up, lats engaged. Push the floor away. Lock out with glutes.

### B1. Overhead Press (Barbell)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 6–8 | 2 min | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Slight lean-back then snap hips under the bar once past your forehead. Full lockout. Brace core throughout.

### B2. Dumbbell Row (Single Arm)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 8–10 per arm | 90 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Hand and knee on bench. Row to hip, not shoulder. Squeeze at top. Full stretch at bottom.

### C1. Goblet Squat (Kettlebell)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 12–15 | 60 sec | 7 |

**Cues**: Hold kettlebell at chest. Elbows between knees at bottom. Upright torso. Full depth.

### C2. Push-Ups (Weighted or Elevated)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 12–15 | 60 sec | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Hands just outside shoulders. Body straight line. Chest to floor. Full lockout.

### D1. Farmer's Walk

| Sets | Distance | Rest | RPE |
|------|----------|------|-----|
| 3 | 40 meters | 60 sec | 7 |

**Cues**: Heavy dumbbells or kettlebells. Tall posture. Shoulders packed down. Steady pace. Grip hard.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Cat-cow: 1 × 10
- World's greatest stretch: 5 per side
- Child's pose: 45 seconds
- Deep breathing: 1 minute
