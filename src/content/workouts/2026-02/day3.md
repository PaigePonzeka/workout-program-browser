## Warm-Up (10 min)

- Bodyweight squats: 2 × 15
- Walking lunges: 1 × 10 per side
- Leg swings (front-back & lateral): 10 per direction per leg
- Glute bridges: 2 × 12
- Empty bar squats: 1 × 10

## Main Work

### A1. Barbell Back Squat

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 6–8 | 3 min | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Break at hips and knees simultaneously. Knees track over toes. Hit parallel or below. Brace core hard.

### A2. Romanian Deadlift

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 8–10 | 2 min | 7–8 |

**Cues**: Soft knees, hinge at hips. Bar stays close to body. Feel the stretch in hamstrings. Squeeze glutes at top.

### B1. Leg Press

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 10–12 | 90 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: Feet shoulder-width, mid-platform. Full range of motion. Don't lock out knees. Controlled tempo.

### B2. Walking Lunges (Dumbbell)

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 10 per leg | 90 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: Upright torso. Front knee tracks over toes. Push through front heel. Alternate legs.

### C1. Lying Leg Curl

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 3 | 12–15 | 60 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: Hips pressed into pad. Curl fully. 2-second negative. Don't let the weight slam.

### C2. Standing Calf Raises

| Sets | Reps | Rest | RPE |
|------|------|------|-----|
| 4 | 15–20 | 45 sec | 8 |

**Cues**: Full range of motion — deep stretch at bottom, full squeeze at top. 1-second hold at top.

## Cooldown (5 min)

- Quad stretch (standing): 30 seconds per leg
- Pigeon pose: 45 seconds per side
- Hamstring stretch (seated): 30 seconds per leg
- Deep breathing: 1 minute
