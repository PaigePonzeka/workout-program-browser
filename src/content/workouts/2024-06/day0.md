    # Lion Press Pride
    A push-day designed to unleash kingly upper body strength through classic compound presses and wild animal-themed conditioning.
    --- 
    ## Warm-Up
5 min treadmill or row at easy pace
Then 2 rounds (for quality):
- 10 banded pull-aparts
- 10 arm circles each direction
- 8 scap push-ups
- 10 push-up to Downward Dog

## Strength or Skill
Barbell Bench Press
- 4 sets x 8 reps @ RPE 7
- Rest 2-3 min

Dumbbell Seated Shoulder Press
- 3 sets x 10 reps @ RPE 7
- Rest 90 sec

Incline Push-Ups (feet on box)
- 3 sets x 12 reps (scale to regular push-ups if needed)

## Metcon
"Savanna Circuit" (AMRAP 12 min):
- 10 push-ups
- 12 kettlebell swings (moderate)
- 14 box jumps (20/24")
- 30 sec bear crawl (down gym and back)
Scaling: Knee push-ups, step-ups, lighter kettlebell
Time Cap: 12 min

## Cool-Down
- Overhead triceps stretch: 40 sec each
- Child's pose: 60 sec
- Doorway pec stretch: 40 sec each

## Core / Optional Finisher
3 rounds (not for time):
- 15 hollow rocks
- 20 alt. V-ups

## Coaching Notes
Prioritize good pressing form and full lockout on every rep. Keep the pace high in the Metcon, but only if quality can be maintained.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Base weights, 4x8 pressing, 12 min AMRAP
- Week 2: Add 5-10 lbs or +2 reps/press set, 14 min AMRAP
- Week 3: Increase to 5x6 pressing sets, heavier KB or box, 12 min AMRAP
- Week 4: Deload (drop all loads by ~10%), 10 min AMRAP
