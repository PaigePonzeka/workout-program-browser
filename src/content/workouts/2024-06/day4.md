---# Full Body Forest Frolic
  A playful full-body blend mimicking a nimble trip through the wild, combining push, pull, and legs for a high-volume finisher.
  ## Warm-Up
5 min row/ski/bike
Then 2 rounds:
- 10 PVC passthroughs
- 10 kangaroo hops (vertical hops)
- 10 bodyweight good mornings

## Strength or Skill
Alternating Circuit (3 rounds for quality):
- 10 DB push press (moderate)
- 10 pull-ups or banded rows
- 16 alternating walking lunges (DBs optional)
Rest as needed between rounds

## Metcon
"Frolic Flow" (20 min cap chiller):
4 rounds for time:
- 250m row/bike or 400m run
- 20 Russian kettlebell swings
- 15 push-ups
- 10 DB goblet squats
Scale: Lighter DB/KB, knee push-ups, march instead of run
Time Cap: 20 min

## Cool-Down
- Standing quad + opp. arm stretch: 30 sec/side
- Shoulder/pec wall stretch: 45 sec/side
- Frog pose: 40 sec

## Core / Optional Finisher
Not for time: 3x
- 15 deadbugs
- :30 hollow hold

- Week 1: All as written, aim for completion
- Week 2: Add round or increase loading (lightly)
- Week 3: Reduce rest time between circuit rounds
