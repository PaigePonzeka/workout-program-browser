---# Cheetah Chase
  Engine/conditioning day built for pure speed and stamina—light, fast, and fun, channeling your inner cheetah for caloric burn and lung power.
  ## Warm-Up
5 min run/bike (easy)
Then 2 rounds:
- 20 jumping jacks
- 10 alternating spiderman lunges
- 15 kettlebell deadlifts (light)

## Metcon
"Cheetah Circuit" (EMOM 18 min):
Minute 1: 12 calorie row/bike or 15 burpees
Minute 2: 15 wall balls (20/14 lbs)
Minute 3: 20 box jumps (step down)
Continue sequence for 6 total cycles.
Scale: Jumping air squats for box, lighter wall ball, reduce reps as needed
Time Cap: 18 min

## Cool-Down
- Supine twist stretch: 45 sec/side
- Cobra stretch: 45 sec
- Standing forward fold: 60 sec

## Core / Optional Finisher
2 rounds:
- 30 sec mountain climbers
- 20 flutter kicks

## Coaching Notes
Move fast but steady—engine work = pace discipline! Keep form sharp under fatigue. This EMOM should be brutal but sustainable.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Prescribed reps, 18 min EMOM
- Week 2: +2 reps per movement if possible, keep pace
- Week 3: Add 20 min EMOM or choose a DB thruster instead of wall balls
- Week 4: 15 min EMOM, reduce reps 10%, focus on speed
