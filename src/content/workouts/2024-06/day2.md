    # Rhino Rampage
    A lower body session bringing rhino-level power for legs and glutes, blending heavy squats, thrusts, and a charging finisher.
    --- 
    ## Warm-Up
5 min stairmaster/row
Then 2 rounds:
- 10 glute bridges
- 10 air squats
- 10 walking lunges
- 15 banded lateral steps

## Strength or Skill
Barbell Back Squat
- 4 sets x 8 reps @ RPE 7-7.5
- Rest 2-3 min

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
- 3 sets x 12 reps

Weighted Bulgarian Split Squat
- 3 sets x 8 reps/leg

## Metcon
"Trample Dash" (AMRAP 15 min):
- 12 goblet squats (moderate KB)
- 10 box step-overs (24/20")
- 12 alternating reverse lunges
- 150m run or 250m row (fast)
Scale: Bodyweight squats/lunges, low box or step-ups, short row/run

## Cool-Down
- Seated hamstring stretch: 45 sec/side
- Quad stretch: 30 sec/side
- Pigeon pose: 40 sec/side

## Core / Optional Finisher
2 sets (not for time):
- 20 bird-dogs
- 30 sec side plank/side

## Coaching Notes
Keep squat depth consistent. Push for controlled lunges on split squats. Conditioning is leg-heavy, pace out the run/row.
### Week-over-Week Progression
- Week 1: Establish baseline, 4x8 squat
- Week 2: Add 5-10 lbs or 1-2 reps/squat, 16 min AMRAP
- Week 3: Back squat to 5x6 @ RPE 8, increase KB, 14 min AMRAP
- Week 4: Deload, 3x8 at -20% weight, 12 min AMRAP
