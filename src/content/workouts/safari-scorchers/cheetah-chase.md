Pure engine! Sprint and recover on your way to peak stamina, unlocking jungle-level speed and endurance.
## Warm-Up

5 min alternating bike & jump rope
Then 2 rounds:
- 10 inchworm walkouts
- 10 jumping jacks
- 10 bodyweight reverse lunges

## Metcon

"Cheetah Chase"
Buy-in: 400m run (row or 500m if inside)
Then, AMRAP in 18 min:
- 15 wall balls (20/14 lbs)
- 12 box jump overs (20"/step ups)
- 15 cal row (or 12 cals bike)

Scaling:
- Use lighter ball, lower box, or step up, reduce cals/round
- Sub treadmill run (400m) if needed

## Cool-Down

- Quad/hip flexor stretch: 1 min/side
- Figure-4 stretch: 1 min/side
- Breathwork: 2 min supine belly breathing

## Core / Optional Finisher

Optional: 3x max hollow rocks (if energy to spare!)
